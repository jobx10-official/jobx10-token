# Introduction

The **JX10 Token** is the official utility token powering the **JobX10 ecosystem**, a platform focused on modern job coaching, digital payments, and community-driven rewards.

JX10 operates on the **Polygon (PoS)** blockchain, offering:
- Low transaction fees  
- Fast confirmation times  
- Reliable network security  

The token is designed for multiple user groups:
- Job seekers  
- Coaching participants  
- Freelancers & self-employed professionals  
- Companies using JobX10 services  
- Community members  
- Token holders & investors  

The goal of JX10 is to build a scalable, transparent, and user-centric digital ecosystem for professional development.
