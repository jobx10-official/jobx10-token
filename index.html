About JobX10

Real-world utility for coaching, training and job preparation.

JobX10 (JX10) is a utility token created to support real-world services in coaching, job preparation and career development. It connects Web3 technology with practical use cases in education and employment.

The token is designed to reward engagement, enable digital payments for coaching programs and serve as a foundation for future tools in the JobX10 ecosystem.

  • Coaching & Training: Use JX10 as a digital payment layer for coaching programs, courses and workshops.
  • Community Rewards: Reward participants, partners and community members for feedback, activity and long-term support.
  • Future Utilities: Planned integrations, dashboards and ecosystem tools aligned with the roadmap towards 2026.
